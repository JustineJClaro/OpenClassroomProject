NJLHS Services

NJLHS Services are usually at school where we're cleaning stuff up, especially the Japanese Rock garden, the Culinary Garden, and the Library. Although this is only one photo, this is what we usually do and it's very repetitive. Sometimes though, we're in charge of building the Japanese things that our Sensei (Advisor) is in charge of. Launchpad

Launchpad I felt was the most impactful service I've ever done. What we basically did was to help the Filipino Community Center with reducing their catering setup time and to clean the facility. This took very long and took a lot of planning to be succesful. This service took about 57 hours. Despite the long hours, we were extremely successful as our objective made the Filipino Community Center's system be 75% more effieient. After completing the service, I was awarded with a $5,000 scholarship as I showed the best work ethic, cooperation, and leadership. I've never knew I could do something really amazing until the time I took part in Launchpad.
