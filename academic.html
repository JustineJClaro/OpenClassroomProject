National Japanese Language Honor Society

I was a member of the National Japanese Language Honor Society. The National Japanese Language Honor Society is an organization for students who excel in their Japanese language class. In this organization, we are doing frequent activities at school or somewhere else on Oahu that's Japanese related. We're able to give a tours to Japanese exhcange students, have field trips, take care of anything Japanese related at Waipahu High School, and even have the opportunity to go to Japan. This organization is similar to Japanese club, but our activities are at a much larger scale and challnges us to hone our Japanese speaking skills by speaking to Japanese speakers.
